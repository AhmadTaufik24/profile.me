A Little About Me

I am a designer who believes in the power of simplicity. With over 5 years of experience, I've had the privilege of working with startups and large companies to create products that are not only beautiful but also intuitive and impactful.

My design process is strongly rooted in user research and empathy, ensuring that every decision is made with the user's best interest in mind. When I'm not designing, you can find me exploring new coffee shops or capturing moments through my camera lens.

How I Work

Every great project starts with a well-structured process.

01. Discussion & Concept

We start with an in-depth discussion to understand your vision and goals, then design the right creative strategy.

02. Execution & Design

I will start the creative process, from photography, retouching, or design, with regular updates to you.

03. Revision & Finalization

You will review the results. We will make revisions if necessary until you are 100% satisfied with the final result.

04. Delivery & Support

After everything is finished, I will send all the high-quality final files to you and am ready to help if there are any questions.

Frequently Asked Questions

Find quick answers to common questions about my services and work process.

The process starts with a consultation to understand your needs, followed by sending a proposal and offer. After an agreement, we will enter the project execution phase with regular communication until the project is completed and delivered.

The completion time varies greatly depending on the project's complexity. A logo design project usually takes 1-2 weeks, while a photo retouching project can be completed in a few days. A more accurate time estimate will be provided in the proposal.

For new projects, I usually require a 50% down payment before work begins. The remaining payment is made after the project is completed and before the final files are delivered. I accept payments via bank transfer.
